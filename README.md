# Life Leak Tracker
A hyper-minimalist tool to visualize the "leakage" of your life caused by daily time-wasting habits.

## 🚀 Concept: Stop the Leak
"It's just 30 minutes of scrolling." 
When that habit is multiplied by years, it turns into months or even years of your life lost forever. This tool calculates that hidden cost instantly, giving you the reality check needed to reclaim your time.

## ✨ Features
- **Zero Friction:** No login. No tracking. No ads. Just the hard truth.
- **Dynamic Conversion:** Automatically switches units from "Full Days" to "Full Years" to match the scale of your loss.
- **Life Impact Alerts:** Provides direct feedback based on the total time leaked (e.g., "You are losing YEARS of your life.").
- **Period Persistence:** Remembers your tracking period (Years) in your browser for consistent self-reflection.
- **Privacy First:** All calculations stay in your browser. Your habits are your business.

## 🛠 How to Use
1. **Daily Waste:** Enter how many minutes you "leak" per day (social media, procrastination, etc.).
2. **Period:** Enter how many years you intend to (or have) let this habit continue.
3. **The Wake-up Call:** See exactly how many 24-hour days or years of your life are slipping away.

## 📐 The Philosophy
We often protect our money but neglect our time, which is our only non-renewable resource. By visualizing "Time Spent" as "Life Lost," this tool helps you develop a healthy protectiveness over your hours. Stop the leak, start living.
